My earliest memory is of stealing.
Stealing a small plastic rosary, later draping its string of white beads around my neck. I didn't understand what I had taken from the other girl. It weighed almost nothing in my hands.
I threw away the rosary that same afternoon, before anyone else had realized what I had done.
